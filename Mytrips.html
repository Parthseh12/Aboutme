My trip to Banglore

Hi guys this is me Parth Sehgal, so this is about my trip to Banglore So on 23 december 9am was our flight to Banglore. I was going with my dadi and dadi mami. We reached Dwarka New delhi airport at 7am.We boarded the flight at 8:50am. The flight landed around two and a half hour later at around 2-2:30 the flight reached at the Bangalore Airport.Then we went to my Bua's house,We stayed there that night,Then the next morning we went to a resort in Chikmanglore.We reached the resort at around 1pm then we did our lunch and Jumped in the pool.The next day,It was christmas we went for trecking and while coming back we went to a waterfall,then later that night we had a BBQ near the pool.The next day after lunch we headed back to my bua's house.Then the next day in evening we went to a market near by after we reached back to bua's house me,ruhani and her friends went for playing badminton in the badminton court.On 29th December we went to a mall.After that on new year there was a party in their house,We enjoyed a lot in the party.Then on 2nd January was birthday of my dadi mami so we went to a restaurant We had a South Indian breakfast then later that evening we ordered a cake for celebration.Next day we started packing to come back to Delhi.The next day we arrived at the airport at around 9 o'clock the flight the boarding time of the flight was 11:20 a.m.At around 3:30 we reached delhi where my dadumama came to pick us.So the journey was very excited I had a lot of fun in the journey I hope you enjoy this blog.
